Carbonara

Description

One of Italians' favourite

Born in Rome and now worldwide popular it makes us all touch the stars with the creamy egg and pecorino sauce in contrast with the salty and crunchy of the guanciale.

Don't be scared the making process is quick and easy, you just need to keep reading..

Ingredients (4ppl)

  • 400 gr Spaghetti
  • 4 Egg Yolk
  • 100 gr Guanciale
  • 50 gr Pecorino Cheese
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put a pot of water on the gas to boil
  2. Slice the guanciale in small dices
  3. Put the guanciale in a pan and brown it for about 15 minutes (pro tip: don't wash the pan just yet)
  4. Salt the water that should be boiling by now and add the spaghetti (pro tip: take them out al dente)
  5. Add yolk in a bowl and add nearly all the pecorino cheese
  6. Season with black pepper and whip (pro tip: add a tablespoon of cooking water to make it creamier)
  7. Drain the pasta directly into the pan with guanciale (reheat the pan) and stir
  8. Take the pan off the gas, add the sauce from the bowl and stir (pro tip: add more pasta water to make it even more creamier)
  9. Put the pasta into plates and finish it with the pecorino cheese that we left out
  10. Enjoy!
