Description

Spain's Ace in the Hole

Quick to make, perfect for aperitifs, picnics or any occasion where you can't reheat your meal!

Ingredients (4 ppl)

  • 500 gr Potatoes
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • Parmesan Cheese to taste
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and slice them in thin wheels
  2. Put the potatoes in a pan and fry them
  3. Add eggs, cream and parmesan cheese in a bowl and stir manually
  4. Grease a pan with butter or olive oil
  5. Put both the eggs and the potatoes in the pan and cook at a low temperature
  6. After 10 minutes turn the omelette the other way around and wait another 10 minutes
  7. You can either have it freshly made or wait for it to cool down
  8. Enjoy!
