Meatballs

Description

To be Meat and not to Ball

Everybody knows what they are and probably ate them already, but does everyone knows how to prepare this fast but delicious recipe? Follow these steps:

Ingredients (4ppl)

  • 500 gr Beef Mince
  • 1 egg
  • 100 gr Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 glass Milk
  • 50 gr No Crust Bread
  • 20 gr Flour
  • Pepper to taste
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the milk in a bowl and leave the bread inside to soak the most of it
  2. In another bowl put the egg, the parmesan cheese, the beef mince and start mixing
  3. Add the milk soaked bread, some pepper and salt and keep mixing
  4. Divide the mix into small portions and roll those into bowls
  5. Bread the balls into the flour
  6. Add some frying oil in a pan and heat it up
  7. Pan fry the meatballs on medium fire for about 20 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
